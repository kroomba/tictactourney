Wednesday December 6, 2023

Organized by Chris Morant, Chelby Neumann, Draven Moreno, Tony Barajas, and Mason Holland

Time: 3:45 PM

Location: N building tables (in between i and L buildings)

Endorsed by Mr. Bonilla’s BHS Esports program!

Play Tic Tac (Toe) in an overly hype and intense competitive tournament for a potentially funny prize!

RULES: We are playing with competitive rules, not street rules. This is like the “Final Destination, Fox, No Items” of Tic Tac. It will be intense, it will be hype. The winners will receive a very cool prize (trust me)!

Format: Three Player Matches, Tournament Bracket

GUIDELINES:

  • Players face each other 1v1v1. Player order (who is X, O, and Z) is determined by Rock Paper Scissors (Best of One).
  • Squares cannot be taken over more than once by each player.
  • There is only one winner each round-- whoever gets 2 in a row.
  • Good Sportsmanship
  • Don’t Lose
